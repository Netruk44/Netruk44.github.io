## Description

Aim to make big combos and break as many blocks at once as you can. Strategically plan your moves to complete challenges which unlock mysterious portals that you can walk around to view from any angle. Compete against other players to score the most points on the daily challenge leaderboard.

### The free version contains
- Access to the daily challenge mode with today's challenge
- The first tier of levels and related challenges, with 3 different stages to try out and 8 challenges to complete.
- One unlockable portal to view

### The full version contains these additional features
- Full daily challenge history, go back and play any previous challenge.
- An additional 2 tiers of levels and related challenges for you to complete.
- An additional 2 unlockable portals you can view by moving around your space.
- There may even be additional unlockables for those who unlock all 3 portals as well...

### Game Features
- Simple match 3 gameplay
- Fun and engaging challenges
- Unlockable content
- Daily randomized level with leaderboards
- Colorblind and motion accessibility settings
