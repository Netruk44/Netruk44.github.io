EV Charging Calculator is the simplest way to estimate how long it will take to charge your electric vehicle. Whether you're plugging into a standard wall outlet, a Level 2 home charger, or a DC Fast Charger, get instant answers on when you can get back on the road.

### Key Features
*   **Instant Estimates:** Just enter your current battery % and target %, and see exactly how long charging will take.
*   **Smart Finish Time:** Know precisely when your car will be ready ("Tomorrow at 7:30 AM").
*   **Vehicle Profiles:** Save multiple cars with different battery sizes. Perfect for multi-EV households!
*   **Global Voltage Support:** Built-in presets for North American (120V/240V) and International (230V) voltages.
*   **Customizable Chargers:** Select from common presets (Level 1, Level 2, DCFC) or enter a custom kW power rate.
*   **Simple & Clean:** A distraction-free interface designed for quick calculations.
